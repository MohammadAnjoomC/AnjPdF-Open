# AnjPdF Open - Quick PDF Hosting, Share & Transfer Tool 🚀

I built this in **2025** while I was a **Class 11 (Plus One)** student. I am writing this to explain why I made this and how it helps students like me.

### 💡 Why I built this? (A Message to the Future)
I don’t know who is reading this right now, or what year it is for you. Maybe when you read this, this tool is no longer needed. But please, **don't laugh, don't cry, and don't judge.** In Kerala schools, phones are usually not allowed. This makes it hard to transfer study notes to a shopkeeper's computer for printing when you don't have a phone or USB drive.

**The Reality:** I once got into trouble and was even beaten by my teacher because I couldn't get my notes printed on time. I had no device to transfer the file. I felt so low that day. I promised myself I would solve this. I turned my pain into this tool so I could host my files at home and open them at any shop via a URL. **I hope whoever is reading this never has to face a situation like mine.**

### ⚡ Main Features
1. **Host:** Save your PDFs online safely from any device.
2. **Transfer:** Move files to a shop PC without a phone or USB.
3. **Print:** Just open your URL at the shop and print instantly.
4. **User Registration & Management:** Members can create accounts, log in, and **manage or delete** their own uploaded files.
5. **Admin Control:** Secure overall management of the platform.

### 🚀 Speed & Flexibility
* **No Login for Guest Users:** Quick access at shops without an account.
* **Member Dashboard:** A dedicated space for registered members to track their files.

### 🛠️ Tech Info & History
* **Original Host (2025):** `https://anj.ct.ws/` (Note: This link may now be expired/inactive).
* **Language:** PHP (Backend logic and secure registration system).
* **UI:** Lightweight for fast loading on old computers.
* **Storage:** JSON-based fast file management.
